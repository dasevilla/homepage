No News is Good News

No News Is Good News is a chrome extension that hides bad news from your Twitter timeline.

It works by crawling a breaking news website & picking up keywords. Then it hides tweets that contain those keywords, before you see them.

I built No News Is Good News because I want to control when & how I'm exposed to "The News," which is usually distressing and upsetting.

Some people argue that we have a duty to consume and even speak out about The News. I disagree with this, and explain my thinking in some detail here.

No News Is Good News lives on GitHub; I invite you to participate in & improve upon the project.
