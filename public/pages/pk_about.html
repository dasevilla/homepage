A prisoner, having secured a pencil and a small scrap of paper, wants to cram in as much text as possible. So he avoids words with ascenders (bdhfjklt) and descenders (jpgpqy).

This is the premise of the Prisoner's Constraint, an Oulipian writing game.

The Prisoner's Keyboard is a customizable creative writing experience. Install the keyboard, then remove particular letters using the lock icon. You can go with the Prisoner's Constraint, a vowel lipogram, or create your own constraint.

If you're having fun and want to share your work, be sure to submit it!
