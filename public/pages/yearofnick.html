Year of Nick

2015 is my year of public, personal, collaborative building.

Is it also the Year of You? Get in touch (nsbarr@gmail.com) and let's jam together.

Here's what the year looks like so far:

Live

  • HAM

    Short for Hard As a Motherfucker, HAM is a methodology for rapidly turning ideas into prototypes. The v1 of HAM lives on GitHub. As projects get completed using HAM, I will launch wegoham.com and iterate on the methodology.

In Progress

  • l8r

    An iOS app that transforms how we store and recall memories. Currently seeking design and development collaborators.

  • The Product Deck

    A deck of cards meant to support -- or replace -- the product manager in your organization.

Backlog

  • PROCEED

    A zine/cookbook for generative poetry. Every entry consists of the poem, the procedure for generating it, and parameters for changing it.

  • WWJD

    An on-the-go messaging app for asking what Jesus would do in your situation. Sort of a reading comprehension test for myself after reading The Gospel in Brief.

  • ohNYC

    A dynamic fridge magnets game for iOS with words populated by a twitter hashtag (#ohNYC is overheard in NYC).
